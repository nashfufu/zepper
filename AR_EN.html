1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your email address, uploaded files (e.g., logos, videos, or 3D models), and communication details when you contact us or use our AR services.

We also automatically collect limited technical data, such as your browser type, device, and anonymous analytics data via Google Analytics, to improve our platform performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our WebAR services;

3. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and Google Analytics to understand site traffic and improve the user experience. Cookies do not store personal information and can be disabled in your browser settings at any time.

4. Data Protection and Security

All data transmission is secured via HTTPS. Files uploaded to Zepper AR are stored in encrypted environments with access restricted to authorized personnel only.

We never sell or trade your information to third parties. External tools used (e.g., Google Analytics, PayPal) follow their own privacy policies and operate independently of Zepper AR.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion or correction of your data by contacting us.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party tools such as PayPal for payments, Tawk.to for chat support, and Google Analytics for usage analytics. Each has its own data-handling practices, and we encourage reviewing their privacy policies.
